⚡️ Active Projects

Colyseus is a Node.js Framework that facilitates authoritative multiplayer game development

It has SDK available for JavaScript/TypeScript, C# (Unity), Lua (Defold), Haxe, and C++ (Cocos2d-X).

The very first version of Colyseus was released on November 2015 and its development is still active to this date.

⏳ Archived projects

HOOK was a RESTful, extendable Backend as a Service written in PHP that provides instant backend to develop sites and apps faster, with dead-simple integration for iOS, Android, JavaScript, and more.

HOOK was developed during my time as an employee at DOUBLELEFT (acquired by CUBOCC / FLAGCX) The core idea behind HOOK was to provide enough power for the client-side to iterate quickly during the development of digital campaigns and websites.

You can think of it as a simpified Firebase where you own your infrastructure.
